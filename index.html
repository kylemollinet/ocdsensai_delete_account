Privacy Policy - OCD SensAI

Last Updated: October 2025

  • Account Information: Email address, display name (via Google Sign-In or email/password)
  • Therapy Data: Chat conversations, exposure therapy sessions, anxiety ratings, progress tracking
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, session duration
  • Device Data: Push notification tokens, device type, app version

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized OCD therapy and support
  • Track your progress and improvement over time
  • Send you notifications about therapy sessions and reminders
  • Improve our app features and user experience

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely using Firebase and MongoDB Atlas
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases with access controls

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and user management
  • MongoDB Atlas: Secure data storage
  • Railway: Backend service hosting
  • Modal.com: Scheduled notification services

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data at any time
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your account and all data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Control: Manage notification preferences and data sharing

Data Deletion

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your user account and authentication data
  • All chat conversations and therapy sessions
  • All exposure therapy data and anxiety ratings
  • All progress tracking and statistics
  • All notification preferences and settings
  • All feedback and support requests

You can delete your account by navigating to the account tab (in chat history hamburger menu), scroll to the bottom, and click "Delete my Data"
